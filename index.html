Our Story

Since we started work in 2001

DZIGNS architecture and interiors is actively engaged in the creation of contextually appropriate solutions within an increasingly changing world. Set up in Bangalore in 2001, it is a design studio focusing primarily on the fields of Architecture and Interior Design. Over the years the studio has thrived with an energetic team of architects led by Ar. Santhosh Kumar.S (Principal Architect).
